Alfredo pasta

Ingredients

  • 250 g dried lasagne sheets
  • 2 chicken breasts (cooked and shredded)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 300 ml double cream (or heavy cream)
  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g Parmesan (grated)
  • 150 g mozzarella (grated)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add garlic, cook 1 min. Add cream and butter, stir until smooth. Mix in Parmesan, season with salt and pepper → this is your alfredo sauce.
  2. Stir shredded chicken into half the alfredo sauce.
  3. In a baking dish, layer alfredo chicken mix, lasagne sheets, plain alfredo sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers. Finish with sauce and cheese on top.
  4. Bake at 180 °C (350 °F) for 30-40 minutes, until golden and bubbling. Rest 5-10 minutes before serving.
