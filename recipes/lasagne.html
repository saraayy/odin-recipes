Lasgagne

Ingredients

  • 250 g dried lasagne sheets
  • 400 g ground beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 can (400 g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g flour
  • 500 ml milk
  • 150 g grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan, cook onion and garlic until soft. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Stir in tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer 15 minutes.
  2. For the white sauce: melt butter in a pan, stir in flour, cook 1 minute. Slowly whisk in milk until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, lasagne sheets, and white sauce. Repeat layers. Finish with white sauce and cheese on top.
  4. Bake at 180 °C (350 °F) for 30-40 minutes, until golden and bubbling. Rest 5-10 minutes before serving.
