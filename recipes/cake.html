Chocolate cake

Ingredients

  • 200 g plain flour
  • 200 g sugar
  • 75 g cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 240 ml milk
  • 120 ml vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 240 ml hot water

Method

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Stir in hot water (batter will be thin).
  3. Pour into a greased baking tin. Bake at 180 °C (350 °F) for 30-35 minutes. Let cool before serving.
